Overview

SAS stands for Statistical Analysis System. And is provided by SAS Labs. SAS is a programmable integrated system of software published by SAS Institute Inc. for accessing, managing, analyzing, and reporting data.

SAS, the makers of SAS, define SAS with the slogan "The Power to Know" (a registered trademark).

SAS Advantages

SAS Is used for Statistical analysis SAS Is used business forecasting, planning & decision support SAS is Platform Independent SAS creates process flows that are reusable, easily modified, and have embedded data quality processing. The flows are self-documenting and support data lineage analysis. SAS meets enterprise visualization and reporting needs SAS Integrates data across environment and is used for Extraction, Transformation & loading. SAS is a suite of Business Intelligence and can compete with Cognos & business object. SAS can be used for Data mining Tool
